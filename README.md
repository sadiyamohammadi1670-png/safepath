## 🌸 What is SafePath?

SafePath is a **women's safety route finder** that computes the
safest path between two locations in a city — not just the fastest.
It factors in lighting, CCTV, police presence, and incident history
to guide women safely through urban spaces.

---

## 💡 How It Works

> **Weight = Distance × (11 − Safety Score)**
>
> Higher safety score = lower cost = preferred route ✨

The algorithm naturally avoids dark, isolated, and unsafe roads
and picks the route that balances distance AND safety.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🗺️ Interactive city map with animated routes
- 🔴 Colour-coded roads — green safe, amber caution, red danger
- 🌙 Night mode — extra penalties for dark roads
- 📹 CCTV & lighting filters
- 👮 Police patrol zone preference
- 📊 Three route options — Safest, Fastest, Balanced
- ⚠️ Live incident alerts near your route
